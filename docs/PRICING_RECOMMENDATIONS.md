## Pricing Strategy Recommendations

### 1. Value-Based Positioning
Position Saurellius as a **premium-feature platform at mid-market pricing**. Our AI capabilities, Digital Wallet, and integrated messaging are differentiators that justify competitive pricing.

### 2. Transparent Pricing
Follow OnPay's model of transparent, published pricing. Avoid hidden fees that frustrate customers (like some enterprise providers).

### 3. Annual Discount
Offer **15-20% discount** for annual prepayment:
- Starter Annual: $399/year (save $69)
- Professional Annual: $799/year (save $149)
- Business Annual: $1,499/year (save $289)

### 4. Free Trial
Offer **30-day free trial** with full features (like Gusto) to reduce friction.

### 5. Migration Incentive
Offer **3 months free** for customers switching from competitors with proof of current subscription.
